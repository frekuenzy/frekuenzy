About Frekuenzy

Bridging Technology and Spirituality

Welcome to Frekuenzy, a digital sanctuary dedicated to the exploration and application of sound frequency for well-being, meditation, and spiritual growth. In a world that is increasingly noisy and chaotic, we provide a tool for users to tune into the frequencies that resonate with peace, balance, and harmony.

"Our mission is to make the healing power of sound frequencies accessible to everyone, everywhere, for free."

Founded in 2024, Frekuenzy began as a simple experiment: could we create a web-based oscillator that was precise enough for scientific curiosity but accessible enough for daily meditation? The answer was yes, and it has since evolved into a comprehensive platform serving thousands of users seeking the Schumann Resonance, Solfeggio frequencies, and custom binaural beats.

Our Philosophy

We believe that everything in the universe is vibration. From the atoms that make up our bodies to the movement of celestial bodies, frequency is the fundamental language of existence. By consciously aligning ourselves with specific frequencies—such as the 432 Hz "universal layout" or the 7.83 Hz "heartbeat of the Earth"—we can potentially influence our physical and mental states.

While we embrace the spiritual aspects of sound therapy, we are also committed to technological excellence. Our signal generator uses advanced Web Audio API technology to ensure that the waves you hear are mathematically pure and stable.

Why Use Frekuenzy?

Unlike simple YouTube videos where audio compression often distorts the integrity of the frequency, Frekuenzy generates audio in real-time within your browser. This means:

  • Zero Compression: You hear the pure mathematical sine wave, not a compressed MP3 recording.
  • Infinite Playback: No looping artifacts or interruptions.
  • Customizability: You aren't limited to presets; you can explore any frequency between 0.01 Hz and 20,000 Hz.
